All about the Beauty

Your beauty never was scary,
Oh, cosmic wonder, so extraordinary,
In your vast and endless space,
A beauty that time cannot erase.

The galaxies dance and twirl,
In a cosmic waltz, a celestial swirl,
The stars, they sparkle and shine,
In a universe that's forever divine.

From the swirling nebulae,
To the planets far away,
The beauty of the cosmos shines,
In a brilliance that's truly divine.

Your beauty never was scary,
A sight so enchanting and so merry,
A universe that's filled with wonder,
In a never-ending cosmic thunder.

So let us gaze upon the stars,
And bask in their glow from afar,
For in the beauty of the cosmos,
We find a light that forever glows.
